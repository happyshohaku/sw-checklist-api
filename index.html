About This Project

Star Wars Book Checklist is a curated database of Star Wars novels, audiobooks, comics, and reference materials. We help fans discover, track, and explore the rich literary universe of Star Wars across all eras and formats.

Our mission is to make it easy for both new and longtime fans to navigate the extensive Star Wars book catalog, from classic Legends stories to the latest canon releases.

What We Track

  • Book Details: Title, author, publisher, publication dates, ISBN/ASIN
  • Timeline Information: In-universe chronology (BBY/ABY)
  • Series & Categories: Book series, eras, and reading order
  • Format Availability: Hardcover, paperback, eBook, audiobook
  • Audio Details: Narrators, publishers, runtime for audiobooks
  • Canon Status: Canon vs Legends designation
